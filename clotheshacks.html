Clothing Life Hacks!

  • Tired of lint and other materials from your clothes on an outfit you have? Just use a razor to take them off. Then after if there are any left overs just use tape.
  • Use a sharpie to cover bleach stains
  • In need of jeans but don't have time to try them on? Just get the waistline part of your jeans and put it around your neck to see if it fits.
  • Stop sweaters from shedding by putting them in your freezer.
  • Using dish soap will help remove oil stains.
  • Wash sneakers with baking soda, detergent and a toothbrush to liven them up.
